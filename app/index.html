    Jamie who?

    With a passion for immersive storytelling, Jamie revels in pushing the creative boundaries in films that engage, educate, and impact an international audience.

    From England, yet having visited over half of the world's nations, you’ll find in his work compelling stories of social and environmental purpose, often layered with emotive undertones and the accompaniment of gripping, cinematic visuals.

    An outdoor crackerjack, a self-professed ‘lighting obsessive’ and ‘tech nerd’, he has a reputation for crafting beautiful compositions, and employs his warm personality to dig beneath the surface of any story to the heart of the topic.

    Forte

    With a broad network of top tier brands and highly-experienced, sought-after Directors and DP's, I deliver projects at maximum spec with minimum bother. By taking leaps into bold, heartfelt human storylines, applying unrivalled client communication and combining award-winning talent with industry leading gear, my projects arrive overdelivered, on budget, and on time.
